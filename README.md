📊 Power BI Supply Chain Dashboard
🚀 Project Overview

This project focuses on building an interactive Supply Chain Analytics Dashboard using Power BI. It provides end-to-end visibility of the supply chain process — from suppliers to customers — enabling better business decision-making.

🎯 Business Problem

Companies often struggle to monitor their supply chain efficiently due to scattered data and lack of insights.

Key challenges include:

Supplier delays and performance issues
Overstocking or stock shortages
Shipment delays and logistics inefficiencies
Lack of visibility in customer demand and revenue
💡 Solution

Developed a Power BI dashboard to analyze and track the complete supply chain with real-time insights.

📊 Key Features
📦 Supplier Performance Analysis (Lead Time, Quality)
🏭 Inventory Management & Stock Tracking
🚚 Shipment & Carrier Delay Analysis
👥 Customer Insights & Revenue Analysis
📈 KPI Tracking (Revenue, Profit, Perfect Order %)
🛠️ Tools & Technologies
Power BI
DAX (Data Analysis Expressions)
Data Modeling (Fact & Dimension Tables)
📂 Dashboard Sections
Overview – High-level KPIs and summary
Supplier – Lead time and supplier performance
Inventory – Stock levels and inventory value
Shipment – Delivery performance and delays
Customers – Revenue and order insights
📈 Key Metrics
Total Revenue
Profit
Perfect Order %
Inventory Value
Supplier Lead Time
